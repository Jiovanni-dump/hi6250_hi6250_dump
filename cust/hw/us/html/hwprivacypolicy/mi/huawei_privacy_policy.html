Kaupapahere Tūmataitinga a Huawei - Te Hononga o Amerika

 

Whakahōunga Whakamutunga: Huitanguru 2018

Mā tēnei Kaupapahere Tūmataitinga (te "Kaupapahere") e whakamōhio atu nei mātou, a Huawei Device USA, Inc. (huihui katoa me ō mātou hoa kamupene, ko "Huawei", ko "mātou" rānei) i a koe mō te āhua o tā mātou kohikohi me te tukatuka i ō raraunga me ngā mōhiohio whaiaro. Me mōhio koe e hāngai ana tēnei Kaupapahere ki hihonor.com/us, tētahi atu o ngā tauwāhi tuihono, taupānga me te pāpāho pāpori (huihui katoa, ko "Ngā Pae"), ngā hua tāhiko kiritaki a Huawei kua mokoia ko "Huawei", "Honor" rānei ("Ngā Hua"), me ngā ratonga, pūmanawa hoki e whakaratoa ana e Huawei me ngā rōpū kē tauwhāiti ("Ngā Ratonga") e tūhono ana ki Ngā Hua. Ka whakarato pea a Huawei i ngā mōhiohio ki a koe e pā ana ki ngā kaupapahere tāpiri e hāngai ana ki Nga Pae tauwhāiti. Ko Ngā Pae, Ngā Ratonga rānei, (hei tauira, ngā pūrere tauwhāiti, ngā pūnaha mahi, taupānga rānei) ngā wāhanga rānei, e āpiti ana ki tēnei Kaupapahere ka whakamōhio i a koe ina kohikohi ana a Huawei me te tukatuka i ngā mōhiohio mai i aua Pae, Hua, Ratonga rānei. Me aro mai ki ā mātou whakamōhiotanga ina whakamahi ana ā mātou Hua, Ratonga rānei.

1. Whakaae ki te Kaupapahere Tūmataitinga

I ia wā ka uru atu koe, me whakamahi, pūtiro rānei i Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei; pāhekoheko me Huawei; whai wāhi atu rānei ki ngā takahanga, whakatairanga, peti moni, rangahau, uiui, me ētahi atu mahinga e tautokona e Huawei (huihui katoa, ko "Ngā Mahinga"), e tohu ana tō whakaaetanga ki te Kaupapahere o taua wā. Mēnā kāore koe i te whakaae ki te ki tēnei Kaupapahere, kaua e tuku mōhiohio ki a Huawei; te uru atu, whakamahi, pūtirotiro rānei i Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei; te whakauru rānei ki Ngā Mahinga.

2. Ngā Momo Mōhiohio e Kohia Ana e Mātou

E kohia ana e mātou ētahi mōhiohio e mōhiotia ai koe, ā, kāore e whāiti ki ngā mōhiohio ka taea te whakamahi takitahitia, me ētahi atu mōhiohio rānei hei tautuhi, whakapā, kimi rānei i tangata kotahi, te tautuhi i tētahi tangata rānei. Kei roto i tēnei ko te ingoa tuatahi, whakamutunga hoki, ngā wāhitau īmēra, tukunga me ngā wāhitau tuku nama, tau waea, tau waeahiko, ingoa kaiwhakamahi me ngā kupuhipa, rā whānau, hītori whakahoki moni, te wāhi ake, whakatautau tata rānei o tō Hua, ngā mōhiohio whakahoki moni me te tapitapi e pā ana ki tō Hua, ngā kōrero, urupare rānei e pā ana ki Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei, me ētahi atu mōhiohio ehara i te tūmatanui. Ka whakamahia e mātou ngā mōhiohio mō ngā kaupapa rerekē, hei tauira, he tukatuka i tō ota, hei tuku i ngā karere mākete hāngai me te tono i a koe ki ō Mahinga, mā ētahi atu tikanga hoki e whakamāramahia atu anō i raro nei.
I te mea he tika mō te nuinga o ngā paetukutuku, ka kohia aunoatia e mātou ētahi mōhiohio tauwhāiti. Kei roto i tēnei, ka mutu kāore i te whāiti ki ngā mōhiohio e kore e tāutua koe e pā nei ngā mōhiohio ki a koe, pērā i ngā mōhiohio kua whakahiatotia e Huawei, he rōpū kē, ngā mōhiohio rānei kāore nei i te hono ki ngā mōhiohio whakapā, ētahi atu mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro rānei. Kei roto pea i ngā mōhiohio i kohia aunoatia ko ngā mōhiohio mō tō whakamahi i Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga, pērā i tō(ō) wāhitau KI, tatauranga whakamahinga, tatauranga mahinga, hītori pūtirotiro me te whakaterenga ina toro ana ki Ngā Pae me ngā tauwāhi matawhenua arowhānui pērā i te tāonenui e tūhono ana tō rorohiko, pūrere rānei ki te Ipurangi, ngā whārangitukutuku i tonoa, i toroa hoki, ngā waitohuwā, ngā pae i toro koe i mua, i muri hoki i te toro i Ngā Pae, te momo pūnaha mahi ka whakamahia e koe ki tō rorohiko (hei tauira, Microsoft Windows, MAC OS rānei), pūrere rānei (hei tauira, iOS, Android rānei) hei uru ki Ngā Pae, te momo pūtirotiro e whakamahia ana e koe (hei tauira, Firefox, Chrome, Internet Explorer, Safari), te ingoa puni o tō kaiwhakarato ratonga ipurangi, me ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki tō tauwāhi Ipurangi mēnā, ina hoki ka kite koe i ngā pānui hokohoko a Huawei, ā, mēnā ka pāwhiri koe i taua pānui hokohoko. Ka kohia ēnei mōhiohio hei tātari i ngā ia kei te hiatonga me te whakahaere i Ngā Pae.
I tua atu i tēnei, ka kohia pea e Huawei ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki ō tau, ira tangata, waehere poutāpeta, mahi, kaingākau, manakohanga hokonga, manakohanga reo, TT pūkete me ngā kupuhipa mō Ngā Pae, me ngā ihirangi ka uru atu koe, te tiro, pāwhiri, rapua rānei ki Ngā Pae, te pūrere e uru atu ana koe ki aua ihirangi me te roa o te wā e tirotiro ana koe i aua mōhiohio e pā ana ki te maha o ngā wā ka tiria e koe ngā ihirangi kei Ngā Pae mā ngā mono-mai pāpori pērā i a Pukamata, Tīhau, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, LinkedIn me Google+ me ētahi atu mōhiohio e pā ana ki te tiritahi i aua ihirangi, mōhiohio e pā ana ki ngā mea i hokona mai e koe, i raua rānei ki tō kāta hokohoko i a koe i te Pae, te āhua o tō pāhekoheko ki ngā ihirangi mā Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei, ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki ngā mea e uru atu ana koe, tāuru, tiaki, tuku, whiwhi hoki/rānei mā Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei, me ētahi atu mōhiohio mō Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga me tō whakamahi i aua mea, e ai ki ngā kōrero i raro. Ki te mea ka mōhiotia tō ingoa, ngā hoapā mōhiohio rānei nā te āhua o te pupuri i ngā mōhiohio, ko te whakarite he mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro; ki te kore, ko te whakarite he mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro-kore; ā, ko te whakarite kē mō aua mōhiohio he mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro-kore. Ka whakakotahitia pea e Huawei ngā mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro i kohia mā Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga, ā, me ngā mōhiohio e wātea ana mai i ētahi atu pūtake (pērā i ngā pūkete tuimotu, ngā mōhiohio rānei e wātea tūmatanui ana). Ko tā mātou whakarite i ngā mōhiohio i whakakotahitia he mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro.

3. He Pēhea te Kohikohi i Ō Mōhiohio

• Ngā Mōhiohio Tāutunga Whaiaro Ka Tukuna Mai E Koe, E Whakaputaina Ana e Ō Mahinga

Ina whakamahia e koe Ngā Pae, kei roto i Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga ko ētahi mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro ko kohia e mātou mōu. Hei tauira, ka tukuna mai pea e koe ngā mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro ina hangaia e koe he pūkete, kōtaha rānei kei Ngā Pae, kei tō Hua, Ratonga rānei, te hoko Hua, Ratonga rānei, te rēhita, te whakahoki rānei i ō Hua, Ratonga rānei, ka whakaritea ō hokonga, ka tukuna mai ō Hua, Ratonga rānei, te mahia te whakahohe, manatoko rānei i te Hua, Ratonga rānei, te huri, te whakamahi rānei i tō pūkete Huawei, te tono me te whiwhi tautoko hangarau, ngā whakahōu rānei kua whakamanahia, te whakawhitiwhiti, pāhekoheko rānei me te ratonga kiritaki, tētahi atu kaimahi Huawei, te whai wāhi ki tētahi Hohenga, ina tukuna mai rānei e koe ētahi atu mōhiohio mā Ngā Pae, tētahi atu āhuatanga rānei. Kei roto pea i ēnei mōhiohio ko tō wāhitau tukunga, tuku nama rānei, TT kaiwhakamahi, kupuhipa, tau ota, tau waea, ō taipitopito utunga ina ota ana mai koe i Ngā Pae, tō wāhitau īmēra, ira tangata, rā whānau, ngā manakohanga whaiaro tauwhāiti, me ētahi atu mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro kei roto i te Wāhanga 2 i runga. Waihoki, kei a koe pea te kōwhiringa ki te pūrua i tō Hua ki tētahi Ratonga kapua a Huawei, ā, i reira ka tiria pea ētahi mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro tauwhāiti me Huawei.
Ka puta pea i ngā mahi ka oti i a koe pērā i te hoko mai i Ngā Pae, te whakahoki i tētahi Hua, Ratonga rānei, te tono tautoko hangarau, ko ētahi mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro ka kohia e mātou, pērā i te tauwhitinga me te hītori ota, ngā tau tono, hītori whakahoki, me ngā tau tīkiti. Ka whakarato pea Ngā Pae i tētahi kōwhiringa ki a koe ki te takiuru ki Ngā Pae mā ō mōhiohio kōtaha mai i tētahi whatunga pāpori e rēhita ana koe. Mā te whakamahi i taua tikanga hei takiuru, ka kohia pea e Huawei tō ingoakaiwhakamahi, īmēra, ētahi atu mōhiohio hoki/rānei e pāhono ana ki tō pūkete ki taua whatunga pāpori e tono ana mātou, ā, e tiri ana koe i a mātou.

• Ngā Mōhiohio ka Kohia mā te Hangarau

Ka kohia pea ō mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro me ō mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro-kore mā ngā hangarau tino whānui, ā, kei raro nei te maha o ērā. Ki te mea ka mōhiotia tō ingoa, ngā hoapā mōhiohio rānei, ka whakakotahitia rānei me ngā mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro nā te āhua o te pupuri i ngā mōhiohio, ko te whakarite he mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro; ki te kore, ko te whakarite he mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro-kore.

o Ngā Pihikete me Ētahi Atu Pūtāutu

Ka whakamahia e Huawei me ana pātui pihikete, ētahi atu hangarau ōrite hei kohikohi mōhiohio ina toro koe i ā mātou Pae ("Hangarau Kohinga"). Ina toro koe i ā mātou Pae, ka rokirokitia e tō pūtirotiro tukutuku ngā pihikete ki tō rorohiko, pūrere rānei, ka tuwhera pea tō rorohiko, pūrere rānei ki ētahi atu Hangarau Kohinga e mahi motuhake ana, i te taha rānei i ngā pihikete. Ka whakahohea e ā mātou pihikete ētahi atu Hangarau Kohinga Ngā Pae hei "whakamaumahara" i a koe, ahakoa i tō toronga kotahi (mā tētahi "wātū pihikete"), mō ngā toronga tāruarua ranei (mā tētahi "pihikete pakepake"), ā, ka whakamahia pea hei tatari i te āhua e pāhekoheko ana koe me ā mātou Pae hei arotau i ā mātou ratonga, hei whakapai ake i tō wheako me te whakarato i ngā pānui hokohoko whaiaro ki a koe. Ka āwhina pea ngā pihikete ki te whakamahara i Ngā Pae ki ō tautuhinga, pērā i te rahi momotuhi, me ētahi atu manakohanga mō tō tiro i Ngā Pae ki tō rorohiko, pūrere pūkoro. Waihoki, ka whirinaki anō ētahi o ngā āhuahira pāhekoheko o Ngā Pae ki te Hangarau Kohinga ka raua e ngā rōpū kē. E whai ake ko ngā tauira o ngā momo pihikete ka whakamahia e mātou, ā, he aha te kaupapa:

  • Ngā pihikete waiwai. Ka whakamahia ēnei pihikete hei takawaenga i ngā taumahinga waiwai kei Ngā Pae, pērā i te pupuri i tō kete hokohoko i roto i te tukanga takiatu, te whakariterite utunga rānei.
  • Ngā pihikete taumahinga. Ka whakamahia e mātou ēnei pihikete hei whakarato i ngā wheako māene kei Ngā Pae, hei tauira, te tiaki i ō manakohanga me te rokiroki i tō kete hokohoko mō ngā toronga ā muri ake.
  • Ngā pihikete mahinga me ngā tātaritanga. Ka āwhina ēnei pihikete i a mātou kia mārama ai ki ngā whanonga o ngā manuhiri ki Ngā Pae hei arotau i ā mātou ratonga me te whakapai ake i tō wheako.
  • Pāpāho pāpori. Ka whakarato pea i Ngā Pae ko ngā mono-mai pāpori ki a koe mai i ngā whatunga pāpori rerekē pērā i a Pukamata, Tīhau, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, LinkedIn me Google+. Ka whirinaki pea ēnei mono-mai ki ngā pihikete, ka taea, i tua atu i ētahi atu mea, te whakamahi hei tuku taumahinga tauwhāiti, engari ka aroturuki anō i tō whanonga ki Ngā Pae, ā, puta noa i te tukutuku. Mēnā ka tāpae mōhiohio whaiaro koe ki tētahi o aua whatunga pāpori, ka whakahaerehia ō mōhiohio e ngā kaupapahere tūmataitinga. E whakatenatena ana mātou i a koe kia āta pānuitia te kaupapahere tūmataitinga o ngā mono-mai pāpori ka whakamahia e koe, ngā paetukutuku rōpū kē rānei ka toroa e koe.
  • Pānui hokohoko. Ka whakamahia ētahi pihikete kei Ngā Pae mō ngā kaupapa pānui hokohoko. Mā tēnei ka taea e koe te whakaatu ā mātou pānui hokohoko ki ngā paetukutuku pātui ki ngā manuhiri e hiahia ana i a mātou hua. E tātari ana ngā hangarau whakahāngai anō i ō pihikete me te whakaatu pānui hokohoko e ai ki tō whanonga tirotiro o mua. E kōrerohia ana i raro ko ētahi atu mōhiohio mō ā mātou hokohoko me te whakamahi i ā mātou hangarau whakahāngai anō.

Ka taea e koe ō tautuhinga pūtirotiro te huri hei muku, ārai rānei i ngā pihikete mai i te rokiroki ki tō rorohiko, pūrere pūkoro rānei me te kore whai whakaaetanga i a koe. o Te Kohinga Aunoa mai i Ngā Pae - Ngā Rangitaki Tūmau/Pūtirotiro

Ki te toro aunoa i Ngā Pae ka tukuna pea ngā raraunga tauwhāiti mai i tō pūtirotiro ki ā mātou tūmau, ā i te taha o ētahi atu take, ka taea ā mātou ratonga te arotau me te whakawhaiaro me te whakapai ake i tō wheako ki Ngā Pae. Kei roto pea i ēnei mōhiohio ko ngā mōhiohio ka tārua e koe ki Ngā Pae, tō wāhitau KI, rā me te wā i toro koe, URL i tukuna (te pae i ahu mai koe) me ngā mōhiohio mō te pūtirotiro i whakamahia e koe (momo pūtirotiro me te putanga, pūnaha mahi, aha atu aha atu), me ētahi atu mōhiohio i kohia aunoatia e ai ki te Wāhanga 2 i runga ake. Me mōhio koe mēnā kei te takiuru koe ki tō pūkete Huawei, Honor rānei, ko ētahi mōhiohio tauwhāiti pērā i ngā ihirangi ka uru atu koe, tiro, pāwhiri, rapu rānei i Ngā Pae, ngā pūrere i uru atu ai koe ki aua ihirangi, te roa o tō titiro i aua mōhiohio, me ngā ihirangi kei tō kāta hokohoko kei Ngā Pae, ka taea pea ērā te tāutu.

• Te Kohinga Aunoa mai i Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga

Tērā pea ka tukuna atu i te rēhitatanga me te whakamahinga o Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga ētahi raraunga tauwhāiti mai i te Hua, Ratonga rānei ki a mātou. Kei roto pea i ēnei mōhiohio ko te tauira pūmārō, wāhitau MAC, tau IMEI, tau IMSI, me ētahi atu pūtāutu kōhure mō Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga, ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki te pūnaha mahi o te Hua me ngā tautuhinga tauwhāiti i tīpakohia e ai ki Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga; ngā taupānga me ngā pūmanawa ka tikiake koe ki Ngā Hua; ko te kaiwhakarato waea pūkoro e whakarato ana i te Hua ki ngā ratonga kawe; ētahi mōhiohio tauwhāiti me ngā ihirangi ka tukuna, i whiwhi hoki/rānei mā Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei; ngā paetukutuku i toroa mā Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei; he pēhea tō pāhekoheko me ngā ihirangi mā Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei; ngā mōhiohio e pā ana ki tāu e uru atu ana, te tāuru, tiaki hoki/rānei mā Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei; me te whakamahinga arowhānui me ngā mōhiohio mahinga me ngā tatauranga e pā ana ki te Hua, Ratonga rānei.

• Ngā Ratonga Tauwāhi mā Ngā Hua me Ngā Ratonga

Ka kohia anō pea e mātou ngā raraunga tauwāhi-rohe mai i Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga ina whakamahia e koe ngā āhuahira ratonga ā-tauwāhi tauwhāiti, arā, ina rapu koe i ngā āhuatanga huarere i ngā tauwāhi whenua ā-rohe, me ngana rānei ki te aroturuki i tētahi Hua i ngaro, i whānakotia rānei mā te tauwāhi-rohe. Kei roto anō i ngā mōhiohio ka kohia mā ēnei āhuahira ratonga ā-tauwāhi ko te TT, te momo me te tauira o te Hua, me ngā mōhiohio tauwāhi-rohe wā tūturu o te Hua (me ngā mōhiohio Global Positioning System ("GPS"), Wi-Fi, tau TT rānei o te pourewa tohu o tō whatunga kaiwhakarato waea pūkoro). Ka taea e koe ō mōhiohio GPS te whakakā, whakaweto rānei mā te haere ki ngā tautuhinga GPS kei te Hua, engari kāore i a koe te motika ki te whakahere i te Wi-Fi me ngā tau TT o te Hua, te pourewa tohu rānei o tō whatunga kaiwhakarato waea pūkoro i te mea kei runga tēnei i te kaupapahere a tō kaiwhakarato waea pūkoro.

Kāore e whakaoatitia ka tino haumaru te tarenga o ngā raraunga mā te ipurangi. Ka taea pea e ngā rōpū kē kaore i raro i te mana o Huawei te haukoti, te uru poka noa rānei ki ngā tarenga, ngā whakawhitiwhiti tūmataiti rānei. Ahakoa ka whakapau kaha mātou ki te pare i ō mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro, e kore e taea e Huawei, e ā mātou kaiwhakarato ratonga rānei te whakaū, te pūtāhui rānei i te haumarutanga o ngā mōhiohio ka tarea e koe ki a mātou mā te ipurangi. Kei runga koe aua tarenga. Ko ngā mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro ka kohia e Huawei ka rokirokitia, ka tukatukahia pea ngā mōhiohio ki Te Hononga o Amerika, Tiamana, tētahi atu whenua rānei e whakahaere whakaurunga a Huawei, ōna hoa pakihi, kaiwhakarato ratonga rānei.
Mā te whakamahi i Ngā Pae, Ngā Hua, Ngā Ratonga rānei, e āta whakahua ana koe i te tarenga o ngā mōhiohio i waho o Te Hononga o Amerika. Ahakoa ka whai whakaarohia e matou tō tūmataitinga, e tūtohu ana mātou ki te whakarite kua tautuhia anō tō pūrere, ā, kua ūkuia ngā raraunga katoa mō tō whakahoki atu i tō Hua mō ngā tapitapi, te whakahoki rānei.
Ka puritia e Huawei ō mōhiohio tāutunga whaiaro e ai ki ana kaupapahere pūrongo ā-roto, tukatuka, pupuri hoki me ngā ture hāngai. Ka puritia pea e mātou ō mōhiohio mō te roanga e hohe ana tō pūkete, e hiahiatia ana rānei hei whakarato ratonga ki a koe, te ū ki ō herenga ture, te whakatau wenewene me te whakaū i ā mātou whakaaetanga.
